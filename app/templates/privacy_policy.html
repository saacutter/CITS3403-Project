Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [10/05/2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tournament Manager. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

2. Information We Collect

To effectively operate Tournament Manager and improve your experience, we may collect several types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, username.
  • Tournament Data: Player rankings, match results, game history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, session activity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect and process data to provide tournament management services, improve user experience, ensure security, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your data, but we may share it with tournament organizers, service providers, or legal authorities when necessary.

5. Data Security

We implement encryption protocols, restricted access measures, and regular security audits to protect your information.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal data and opt out of non-essential communications.

7. Retention Policy

We retain data for operational, security, and legal purposes but anonymize identifiers upon request.
